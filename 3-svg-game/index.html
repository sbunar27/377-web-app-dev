Instructions and Rules

Basic Controls

1. Press the start button to begin the game

2. Enter a bet between 5 - 50

3. You must hit 'HIT' twice to get your first two cards.

The onscreen message will disappear when this is completed.

4a. If you would like to draw a card, click 'HIT!'

4b. If you would like to hold your current hand and wait, click 'STAND!'

5. If you think your score is close enough to 21, hit 'END!' to end the game.

The Rules of the Game

You must get a score the closest to (but not over!) 21 to win.

The dealer also draws cards, so you have to get closer to 21 than they do. You will be able to see the dealer's first card.

If you get a score over 21, that's called a bust (meaning you lose).

Card Values

All cards are at face value, excluding the King, Queen, and Jack

Kings, Queens, and Jacks are all worth 10 points

An Ace will have a value of 11 unless that would make the dealer/player's score go above 21. In that case, it has a value of 1.
