Our Band

The Pride of Hanover Marching Band is a marching band located in Hanover, Massachusetts at Hanover High School. Since its founding, the Pride of Hanover has strived to achieve a sort of enthusiasm and excellence that no other band could match. When in uniform on the football field, we present ourselves as an ensemble with integrity and respect. Though many may not think marching band is a sport, it requires hours of practice and dedication, as well as physical activity. (WIP...)
