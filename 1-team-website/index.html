A Message from The Director

Participation in the Instrumental Music Program at Hanover High School is an incredible opportunity to develop a lifelong relationship with music, collaborate with others, participate in a vibrant community of student musicians, and experience a variety of performance opportunities both locally and abroad. Every student-musician is a valued ensemble member and can grow through consistent practice, peer assistance, and a collaborative and engaging rehearsal environment. Band is a safe place for everyone to thrive and be themselves while working toward a common goal of musical excellence. We value musical expression, individual growth, risk-taking, independent musicianship, and student leadership. At the core of our culture is the phrase, “You’ll Never Walk Alone.”

- Mr. Harden
